Kratos

This is Kratos, son of zeus and the ghost of sparta. He is the protagonist inside a video game series called God Of War.

    Three things I admire about Kratos

  • Arch story, his story begins by his brother being kidnapped. He then goes on a hunt to avenge his brother.
  • Beating death, Kratos do not die, he is not like us instead he fights death and beats it.
  • Family man. Even though he has killed his father, wife and kids, he has gone through change for the better. In the new games, kratos get another son where he tries to slay the demons from the past and becomes a loving family man that sets killing as his last resort.
